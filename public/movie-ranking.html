The Best Movies According to Aaron

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Predestination

For his final assignment, a top temporal agent must pursue the one criminal that has eluded him throughout time. The chase turns into a unique, surprising and mind-bending exploration of love, fate, identity and time travel taboos.

2018

People from all walks of life face catastrophic consequences during devastating floods in 2018. The people of Kerala work together to survive the calamity.

The Platform

In the future, prisoners housed in vertical cells watch as inmates in the upper cells are fed while those below starve.
